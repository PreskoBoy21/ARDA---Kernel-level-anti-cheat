## What entails gameplay experience in regards to cheating.

- **Balance** – It breaks the balance of the game by letting cheaters bypass mechanics or dominate strategies that should be counter able.
    
- **Challenge / difficulty** – Cheating trivializes challenges, removing the sense of accomplishment from gameplay.
    
- **Progression / reward structure** – Cheaters can skip progression systems, making achievements meaningless for everyone else.
    
- **Immersion / enjoyment** – Seeing or being affected by cheaters breaks immersion and reduces overall enjoyment.
    
- **Community / social interaction** – Cheating erodes trust between players, creating frustration and toxicity in the community.
    
- **Competition / skill expression** – Honest players can no longer showcase skill when cheaters overshadow them.
    
- **Consistency / reliability** – Cheating makes game outcomes unpredictable and undermines confidence in the game’s rules and systems.
